About us

We excel at building data systems and analyzing data regardless of its type. While our main focus is the analysis of the molecular data, we have a lot of experience with processing other kinds of tabular data, image data, sound data, and time series for which multiple grants in data science and innovation were secured in the past.

In most cases data preprocessing part needs extra care and attention to detail in relation to the domain. We adapt our approach to embrace the imperative importance of Domain-Driven Design through close collaboration with you. When building your data system, be it just a database or a more complex system with multiple components to integrate, we implement appropriate strategies based on your input and decide on architecture that ensures effective data collection, storage, retrieval and analysis in order to fulfill your business goals and expectations.

We combine our diverse academic background, which includes pharmacy, biochemistry, molecular biology, electronics, mathematics, computer science and programming engineering with our accomplished and seasoned industry background in pharmaceuticals, cheminformatics, applied statistics, data engineering, machine learning and software design.

Despite our full commitment to ongoing projects, we always find time to challenge the latest methodologies and technologies and do our own scientific research. This allows us the application of that same innovation and acquired novel knowledge for designing and creating your solution. Recently, this also led to winning of the EU grant worth $1.5 mil for advanced data science in pharmaceuticals. Initiating and participating in many patent screenings, applications and defenses has taught us how to properly protect intellectual property in a timely manner.
